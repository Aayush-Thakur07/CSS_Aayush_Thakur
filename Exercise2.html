Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Butter
  • Tomato slices
  • Sliced bread

Instructions:

1)Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
2) Place bacon slices in the skillet and cook until crispy, then remove from skillet and drain on paper towels.
3) Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
4) Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of one slice of bread, followed by tomato slices and bacon.
5) Top with another slice of cheese and another slice of bread, buttered side facing out.
6)Place the sandwich in the skillet over medium heat.
7) Cook until the bottom is golden brown, then flip and cook until the other side is golden brown and the cheese is melted. Serve hot and enjoy!
